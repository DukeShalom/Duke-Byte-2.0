The Process

How Duke Byte 2.0 Works

A structured, 8-weeks journey from a beginner to a certified professional.

1

Register & Pay Program Fee

Complete your registration by paying a token amount of NGN20,000 to secure your spot. You'll be added to the Cohort 2.0 WhatsApp community, offered an Admission Letter and gain to access to the Students' Portal on May 25, 2026.

2

Watch Pre-Recorded Modules

Every Mondays, a new 30-45 minutes instructional video drops. Watch at your own pace and take notes. These modules prepares you for the live sessions.

3

Attend Live Sessions & Practicals

Every Thursdays (Q&A) and Saturday (Byte-Sized Master Class), join live Google Meet sessions with expert tutors. Ask questions. Follow through and build.

4

Submit Weekly Assignments

Every Sundays, submit your assignment(s). Get personalized feedback from tutors within 48 hours. Assignments ensure you're actually learning, not just watching.

5

Build Your Capstone Project

Weeks 7-8 are dedicated to your final portfolio project. You'll build something real. Something you can show your employers, potential clients or clients.

6

Graduate & Get Certified

Complete 6/8 sessions, submit 6/8 assignments, finish your project, and pay the Program fee. You'll receive your Duke Academy certificate (digital / physical) and Post program career coaching.
